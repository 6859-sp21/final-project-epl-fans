Know About The Team

Manchester United, the most popular football club in the world

Manchester United Football Club is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, England, that competes in the Premier League, the top flight of English football. Nicknamed "the Red Devils", the club was founded in 1878, changed its name to Manchester United in 1902 and moved to its current stadium, Old Trafford, in 1910.

Manchester United have won more trophies than any other club in English football, with a record 20 League titles, 12 FA Cups, five League Cups and a record 21 FA Community Shields.

Manchester United was the highest-earning football club in the world for 2016–17, with an annual revenue of €676.3 million, and the world's third most valuable football club in 2019, valued at £3.15 billion ($3.81 billion).

Ranking Radar Chart

This chart shows the 20 years of ranking records of top 10 EPL clubs. Each line of circles shows the ranking of that particular year. Starting from the outer circle, it shows high-ranking teams in sequence. If you hover over the club names, you can see the radar chart for the ranking history of that club. A large radar means that the club maintained the high ranking throughout the period.



Winning Points Line Chart


This chart shows the Performance Comparison between Manchester United with other top10 teams in the league. The metric we use is Points. A team is awarded 3 points after winning a match or one point if the game ends as a draw or tie. By selecting different teams, we can compare the performance for them. Without any doubts, Manchester United is among the top performance tier in the league.

Strong Fan Base

Manchester United is the undoubted champion of club social media. In total, Manchester United have an audience of over 123m on social media, vastly more than second place Chelsea (79.1m). Strong, loyal fans are the driving force of team's outstanding performances, which in turn attracts even more fans.

Fans Bubble chart
