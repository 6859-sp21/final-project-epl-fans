Online Course for Women Entrepreneurs

Project Info:

This online course was developed to support the training of UN-APCICT/ESCAP's flagship training programme for women entrepreneurs in developing countries.
The course is consisted of various multimedia materials to support the learning of trainees, such as video lectures, interactive quizzes, etc.
