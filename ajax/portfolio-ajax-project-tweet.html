Turning Tweets into Knowledge

Project Info:

A text classifier that determines whether tweets on the Trump's twitter account are written by Trump himself or his staff. The data is from an online archive at http://www.trumptwitterarchive.com.

Project Details:

  • Organization:MIT
  • Field:Text analytics
  • Technologies:R, Logistric Regression, CART, Random Forest, AUC
